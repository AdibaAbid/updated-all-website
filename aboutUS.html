Who We Are

Welcome to the home of delicious savours.
CHATKHARA MEAL - A quick service restaurant made to look after your hunger pangs.
Our mission is to give our customers the most authentic and artisanal online experience of Food at the best price. All our products – whether it’s bread, biscuits, sandwiches, burgers or pasta – are freshly baked every day using the best natural ingredients and time-honored processes. We know our customers are deeply concerned about what goes into their food so we work hard to ensure that every batch is baked from scratch and is preservative- and additive-free as far as possible.
Delivering with CHATKHARA MEAL , you get flexibility and fair prices.

We Deliver Your Food On Your Doorstep.
